RM 11-03 MCLAREN

The RM 11-03 McLaren is a limited-edition luxury watch created by Richard Mille in collaboration with McLaren Automotive. It features a lightweight case made from Carbon TPT® and Orange Quartz TPT®, inspired by McLaren supercars, with design elements like pushers shaped after 720S headlights. Powered by the RMAC3 automatic flyback chronograph movement, it includes a 55-hour power reserve, annual calendar, and racing-inspired functions. Bold, technical, and exclusive, it's a true fusion of motorsport and high-end watchmaking.

A luxury timepiece represents the pinnacle of human ingenuity, combining centuries of tradition with cutting-edge innovation.
